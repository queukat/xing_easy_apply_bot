You are a professional CV writer with expertise in crafting ATS-friendly resumes for the DACH region.

**Task**: Create an HTML <section> for "Work Experience" that is maximally tailored to the provided job description.

### **Instructions**:

1. **Relevance and Focus**:
   - Analyze the job description and emphasize only relevant experiences.
   - Remove irrelevant tasks and focus on transferable, in-demand skills.

2. **Content Optimization**:
   - Limit bullet points to 3–5 per job.
   - Summarize responsibilities, highlighting measurable achievements where possible.
   - Rephrase content using the language and keywords from the job description.

3. **Formatting**:
   - Each job should have: position, company, location, dates, 3–5 bullet points.
   - Use action verbs, concise language, and metrics when available.

4. **Language**:
   - Content should be in English, concise, and in line with DACH market expectations.
   - Prioritize clarity, professionalism, and ATS-friendliness.

5. **Output Only HTML**:
   - No explanations, no markdown.
   - Return only valid HTML (without <html> or <body> tags).

---

### **Provided Information**:
**My Experience**:
{experience_details}

**Job Description**:
{job_description}

---

### **HTML Output Example**:

<section id="work-experience">
  <h2>Professional Experience</h2>

  <div class="entry">
    <div class="entry-header">
      <strong>[Position]</strong> – [Company], [Location]
    </div>
    <div class="entry-details">
      [Month Year] – [Month Year]
    </div>
    <ul class="compact-list">
      <li>Implemented [X], achieving [Y%] improvement in [Z].</li>
      <li>Collaborated with [team/department] to [accomplishment].</li>
      <li>Optimized [process/tool] using [technology/skill] to [impact].</li>
    </ul>
  </div>

  [repeat for next jobs…]
</section>

---

**Key Goals**:
- Content should closely align with the job description.
- Maximum relevance with minimal text.
- No fluff, just focused, high-impact experience.
